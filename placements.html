Placements at VIT Pune

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune, has a stellar placement record, consistently delivering excellent career opportunities for its students. The institute's dedicated Training and Placement Cell works tirelessly to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring that students are well-prepared for their professional journeys.

In the last academic year, over **250 companies** visited the campus, offering a wide range of job profiles in sectors such as IT, manufacturing, consulting, and core engineering. The highest package offered was **₹44 LPA**, while the average package stood at an impressive **₹9 LPA**. Major recruiters included TCS, Infosys, Amazon, Microsoft, L&T, and Wipro.

VIT Pune ensures students are industry-ready by organizing pre-placement training sessions, mock interviews, and workshops on soft skills. The institute's strong industry connections and alumni network also play a crucial role in its robust placement process.
